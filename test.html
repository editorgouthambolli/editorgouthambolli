PROFESSIONAL SUMMARY

I am a passionate Cinematic Storyteller specializing in turning raw footage into impactful visual narratives. With mastery in Adobe Premiere Pro, After Effects, and Alight Motion, I ensure every frame tells a story with maximum emotional impact.

BEYOND LIMITS: MY JOURNEY

Started with a smartphone in 2018, my 7-year journey is a testament that Ambition > Condition. After mastering mobile editing with 7+ impactful shorts, I transitioned to professional desktop tools, proving that true creativity knows no boundaries.

THE BREAKTHROUGH MOMENT (2022)

Every great story needs a bridge. In 2022, recognized by my best friend and introduced to Director Prithvi, I got my first big break: 'Babu Gari Lokamantha Mayajalam'. This project transformed my home-grown passion into a professional mission.

UNSTOPPABLE PASSION

My journey from crafting unique Chat Edits on Alight Motion to cinematic projects proves that you don't need a high-end studio to create magic. Whether it's a mobile screen or a professional timeline, my work is driven by one core philosophy: "Creativity has no limits when passion leads the way."
